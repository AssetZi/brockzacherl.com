Weight Wingman

Weight Wingman is built around consistency and support, helping people manage weight with less guesswork and more accountability.

Download on App Store Back to Main Site

Why I Built It

Many tracking apps collect numbers but do not reinforce behavior change. I wanted a more supportive system.

Who It Helps

Athletes managing competition weight and anyone building healthier habits.

Core Value

Turns day-to-day effort into a clear, sustainable routine.

What Makes It Useful

  • Prioritizes progress trends over one-off fluctuations.
  • Supports long-term consistency instead of crash approaches.
  • Simple daily flow that reduces decision fatigue.
